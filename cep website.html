Personal & Public Hygiene

Personal Hygiene Essentials

Daily Hygiene Checklist

  • Brush teeth twice daily
  • Wash hands frequently
  • Shower or bathe daily
  • Trim nails regularly
  • Wash hair 2-3 times per week
  • Use deodorant
  • Change clothes daily

Hand Washing Steps

1 Wet hands with clean water
2 Apply soap and lather well
3 Scrub for at least 20 seconds
4 Rinse thoroughly with water
5 Dry with clean towel

How Germs Spread

Direct Contact

Through touching infected surfaces or people

Airborne

Through coughing, sneezing, or breathing

Food & Water

Through contaminated food or water

Public Hygiene Guidelines

In Public Spaces

  • Wear masks when required
  • Maintain social distance
  • Use hand sanitizer frequently
  • Cover coughs and sneezes

Environmental Hygiene

  • Dispose waste properly
  • Keep surroundings clean
  • Avoid water stagnation
  • Maintain green spaces

Preventive vs Reactive Care

Preventive Care

  • Regular health screenings
  • Vaccinations and immunizations
  • Lifestyle counseling
  • Health education
  • Lower long-term costs

Reactive Care

  • Treatment after illness occurs
  • Emergency interventions
  • Higher treatment complexity
  • Increased recovery time
  • Higher medical expenses
